# Olist Customer Retention & Marketing Analytics Dashboard

# Business Problem

Customer retention is a key driver of profitability in e-commerce.
This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior for Olist to identify
retention patterns, repeat purchase behavior, and the key drivers that
influence customer loyalty.

# Tools Used
Power BI,
DAX,
Data Modeling,
Cohort Analysis,
RFM Segmentation

# Key Insights

• Total revenue between 2016–2018 reached $15.8M across 99K orders.

• The repeat buyer rate is only 3.12%, indicating extremely low customer retention.

• Most revenue comes from new customers rather than returning customers.

• Higher review scores strongly correlate with repeat purchase revenue.

• Late deliveries slightly reduce repeat purchase rates.

• Several product categories such as Arts & Crafts and La Cuisine show higher repeat purchase rates.

• RFM analysis shows that lost customers generate the largest revenue contribution,
suggesting high-value customers eventually disengage from the platform.

# Dataset

Dataset: Olist E-commerce Public Dataset
Source: Kaggle
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/olistbr/brazilian-ecommerce
