# Olist-sales-performance-dashboard
This project analyzes e-commerce sales data from the Olist dataset using SQL and Power BI to uncover key business insights related to revenue performance, customer behavior, and seller contributions.
# Objective
Analyze business performance by exploring:
Revenue trends over time,
Top product categories,
Seller performance,
Geographic sales distribution,
Payment behavior.
#Tools
SQL (DBeaver),
Power BI,
DAX.
# Dashboard
$13.2M total revenue across 96K orders,
Health & Beauty is the top category,
São Paulo drives the highest revenue,
Revenue peaks in Nov 2017,
Credit cards dominate payments,
Revenue is concentrated among the top sellers.
# Key Takeaway
A small number of categories, sellers, and regions drive the majority of revenue, highlighting concentration risk and opportunities for targeted growth.
